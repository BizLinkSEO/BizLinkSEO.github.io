What Do I Need to Get Started?

Getting high-quality backlinks is simple. Just follow these 4 easy steps:

1. Submit Your Requirements

To get started, we need a few details from you:

  • Your Website URL – Where should the backlink point?
  • Anchor Text – What keywords should we use?
  • Your Article – If you’ve already written it, send it over. If not, we’ll write it for you!

Share these details by filling out our Google Form or messaging us on WhatsApp.

2. We Analyse & Send Options

We’ll analyse your requirements and handpick a list of high-DA, niche-relevant websites for you to choose from. You’ll get:

  • Website details (DA, traffic, niche).
  • Pricing and turnaround time.

3. Confirm & Pay

Once you’ve chosen a website, confirm your order via WhatsApp or email. We’ll send you secure payment details, and you’re all set!

4. Receive Your Backlink

After your guest post is published, you’ll receive:

  • A live backlink to your website.
  • An SEO report with performance metrics.
